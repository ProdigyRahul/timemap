How TimeMap Works

TimeMap replaces your new tab page with a beautiful visualization of the current year. Each day is represented by a dot in the heatmap - green dots show days that have passed, and empty dots show days remaining in the year.

Year Progress

See at a glance how far you've progressed through the year with our beautiful heatmap display.

Light & Dark Themes

TimeMap automatically adapts to your system's theme preference or you can manually select your preferred theme.

Customizable

Change dot shapes, sizes, and color schemes to match your personal style.

Daily Stats

View days passed, days remaining, and your year completion percentage.

Getting Started

To start using TimeMap, simply open a new tab! Your tab page will be replaced with the TimeMap heatmap.

Click the settings icon in the top-right corner to customize the appearance and behavior of TimeMap.
